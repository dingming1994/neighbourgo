## Codebase Patterns
- EmptyState widget lives at `lib/core/widgets/empty_state.dart`; also re-exported from `loading_overlay.dart` for backward compat
- Use `CachedNetworkImage` (from `cached_network_image` package) instead of `Image.network()` — always provide `placeholder` and `errorWidget`
- When creating TextEditingControllers dynamically (e.g. in a Map), dispose them when removed from the map, not just in `dispose()`
- Profile screens are at `lib/features/profile/presentation/screens/`
- `AppCategories.all` and `AppCategories.getById()` in `category_constants.dart` for category lookups
- `serviceRates` values in Firestore may be String (legacy) or double (new) — always handle both types when reading
- Use `ref.invalidate(provider)` to force-refresh a StreamProvider after a write that should update the UI immediately
- `VerificationBadge` enum and its extensions (`label`, `emoji`) live in `user_model.dart` — use them for badge display
